About


Aakriti Sharma is a B.Arch graduate from Manipal University, Jaipur. Aakriti gained experience while working with Kapadia Associates Design LLP, Mumbai and SRM Architects, Udaipur; before founding her own design company in Jaipur, Rajasthan. In addition to this, she has also worked with an international company, Bouzaine Agence in Tunisia, Africa.

Lines & Circles is a full-service design company. We offer architecture, interior design, graphics, 3D modelling , and turnkey services. We are a multi-disciplinary company with expertise in residential, commercial, multistorey, official, institutional, and hospitality designing.

Our underlying Vision is to create a design which is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functionally efficient at the same time. We create spaces which enhance the experience of the user keeping in mind all the client’s needs and requirements. We hence try to shape our clients imagination using ‘Lines and Circles’ and our creative expertise to turn the design into reality.
